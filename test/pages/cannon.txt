Temps will rise a bit tomorrow and snow is expected to shift to wintry mix &amp; rain so our guess is that first thing in the morning will be your best bet for the best turns. We'll have 50 trails open, around 40 of those will get groomed overnight. 7 lifts are scheduled for Friday including the Aerial Tramway which opens for first tracks at 8:15am while all other main lifts open at 8:30am. Scenic Round-Trip Tram rides for non-skiers are available when the Tram operates. Last ticket sold at 3pm, last car down at 3:45pm. <a href="https://www.cannonmt.com/ski-ride/tickets"><strong>See details</strong></a>. Snowmakers continue to hit some key areas on open terrain and are also working on some new terrain like Short Fuse, Lower Hardscrabble, Red Ball and Turnpike. Please note, Bypass will only be partially groomed with ungroomed snowmaking focused on the steep section (headwall). All ungroomed terrain will be pending patrol inspection and is expert only -please obey all closed trail ropes and signs. Check the <strong><a href="https://www.cannonmt.com/mountain/trails-lifts">Trail &amp; Lift Report</a></strong> for more details on grooming, snowmaking and trail status.<strong><br /></strong></p>

<p><strong>TERRAIN PARKS<br /></strong>The Tossup Park is expected to open today with a mix of Med/Large hits and a selection of rails. There will be a delayed opening while we put the finishing touches on, but get ready for one of the earliest and best openings on the main park! We will keep shaping and adding features as the week goes on.<a href="https://www.cannonmt.com/ski-ride/tickets"></a> <br /><br /><strong>LOOKING AHEAD<br /></strong>Snowmaking and grooming will continue through the week as long as temps allow on Skylight, Lower Hardscrabble, Short Fuse, Lower Ravine, Turnpike, and Brookside and a few guns on Bypass headwall, Big Link and Chute. Next on the list will be the top of Mittersill!</p>
